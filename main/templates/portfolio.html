pyStratego


pyStratego is a cross-platform, online multiplayer version of the popular board game Ultimate Stratego. You (and up to 3 of your friends) can battle it out on the Stratego battlefield from the comfort of your computer chair. Built with the Pygame Python module, pyStratego is cross-platform and runs on Windows, Mac OS X, and Linux. pyStratego's networking capabilities are implemented using the XMPP network protocol. Don't mind the programmer art.

Geist Heist


A 2D isometric stealth action game developed in C# using the XNA framework by a team of 5 Cornell students. I was Project Lead and originally pitched the game concept. Features several levels with AI enemies that are entertaining to interact with. Includes a level editor, allowing players to build their own levels.

Tune Tornado


Tune Tornado allows music enthusiasts to discover new music and recommend it to their friends, and if their friends buy, they get cash rewards. I worked with a team of Cornell students to develop the admin and artist interfaces for the Tune Tornado website. The website was built with Django and deployed on the Google App Engine.
